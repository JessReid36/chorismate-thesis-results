Step 3.2 - L1-budgeted continuous optimizer (placeholder geometry).
Barrier change dDE = sum_i q_i * Dv_i, minimized s.t. sum|q_i| <= Q, sum q_i = 0, |q_i| <= 1.

  Q=2e  -9.27 kcal/mol   (2 charges: +1 cation at grid idx 149 = most-negative Dv, -1 anion)
  Q=3e  -13.76 kcal/mol  (4 charges)
  Q=4e  -18.25 kcal/mol  (4 charges)
  Q=6e  -26.87 kcal/mol  (6 charges)
  Q=8e  -35.15 kcal/mol  (8 charges)
